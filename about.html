Our Values

Our Story

HTML2Canvas was born from a simple need: to capture web content reliably without server-side dependencies. What started as a solution to a common problem has evolved into a comprehensive library trusted by thousands of developers worldwide.

Over the years, we've refined our approach, optimized performance, and expanded capabilities based on real-world use cases and community feedback. From small startups to enterprise applications, HTML2Canvas powers screenshot tools, report generators, and creative applications across the web.

Today, we continue to push boundaries, exploring new rendering techniques and supporting the latest web standards. Our commitment remains unchanged: to provide developers with the best tools for HTML to Canvas conversion.
